About the Author

Katlen M. Loñosa is a 24-year-old Registered Electrical Engineer with a strong commitment to ensuring electrical safety and product reliability. Currently working as a Quality Control Engineer at an Electrical Panel Building Plant, Katlen plays a key role in upholding technical standards and quality benchmarks in the industry.

With hands-on experience as a Test Engineer, she is deeply involved in various testing procedures—ranging from functionality checks to safety validations—of low and medium voltage electrical panels. Her technical expertise and keen attention to detail allow her to identify and resolve issues before they reach the field, ensuring each product meets both client and regulatory requirements.

Driven by continuous improvement and professional growth, Katlen combines her technical knowledge with on-site practical skills, making her a valuable contributor to both engineering teams and quality assurance operations.

When she’s not in the testing area or reviewing technical documentation, Katlen enjoys exploring new developments in electrical systems, mentoring peers, and advocating for excellence in engineering practice.
